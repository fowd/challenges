M1.1 - CTA Promotional Site Selection

Purpose

To research, develop, and design a single site that effective promotes a productâ€”in this case, a book.

Description and Requirements

You are to submit a title for a book or a product you wish to develop a promotional site for. The book or product should allow you to:

  • Show book cover or product image
  • Show a video clip (from YouTube or Vimeo)
  • Purchase the book or product online
  • Have information about the Author/Prodcut
  • Have a synopsis/description
  • Have reviews/testimonials you can quote

Download the Book Promo Project Instructions (PDF)

Note: Make sure you can find a large enough image for the site, as points will be deducted if you cannot provide a clean, large, image of the book or product.

Submission

Submit a compressed folder m1_1-yourlastname, containing:

  • DOCUMENT (PDF, DOC, RTF, or TXT)--save as "information"--with:
    • The synopsis/plot of the book or description of the product
    • Reviews of the book or testimonial(s) of the product
    • Bio of the author or information about the Company/Product
  • IMAGE: image of the book/product you intend to promote
  • LINK: Provide a clickable link to the online video you intend to use in the Online Notes section below.
